The Craft Cycle Creators

We are a group of high school students who coded together at the Girls Who Code 2017 Summer Immersion Program at AT&T Austin. We decided to code this website for our final project to raise awareness on how to properly recycle and show that recycling does not have to be a boring task... instead it can be a fun one! We can prevent the world from getting polluted by paying attention to the little things.
